uint32_t crashpad::MinidumpContextARM::context_flags

A bitfield composed of values of MinidumpContextFlags and MinidumpContextARMFlags.

This field identifies the context structure as a 32-bit ARM CPU context, and indicates which other fields in the structure are valid.
